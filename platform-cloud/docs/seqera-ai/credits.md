Seqera Cloud organizations receive $20 in free credits to get started with Co-Scientist. Credits are billed at the organization level and shared across the organization, with per-user usage limits applied on top of the org pool.

## Check your balance

Run `/credits` in an interactive Co-Scientist session to view your organization's monthly credit balance and usage.

## Request more credits

[Contact us](https://seqera.io/platform/seqera-ai/request-credits/) to request additional credits for your organization.
